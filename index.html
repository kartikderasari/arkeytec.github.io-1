About Us

Who Are We

ARKEYTEC is a computational design firm that offers various services like Architecture, Interior and Landscape design to our client.

We are technology enthusiasts in our hearts. We work with technologies like genetic algorithms, parametrism, Artificial intelligence and machine learning in our project to generate better/efficient designs for our projects as well as those we provide consultations to.

Why Work With Us

At ARKEYETC we believe in providing quality designs, in your budget in the minimum time possible for which we use advance technologies.

We provide one stop solution for all the AEC industry needs.
